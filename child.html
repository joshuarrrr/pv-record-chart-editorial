30 Years of Record-Breaking PV Technology

Any successful photovoltaic technology must satisfy a few key requirements: it must efficiently convert sunlight to electricity; it must be economical to manufacture and maintain; and it must be durable enough to last for a productive lifetime.

With that in mind, Keith Emery, a senior IEEE member at NREL, has spent decades tracking improvements in solar cell efficiency records. He’s shared his his last decade of data with IEEE Spectrum, and we’ve endeavored to make them easier to explore.
